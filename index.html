For Employers

Tools to help you manage your household expenses. While every household is different, for family where both adults work, quite often we give our domestic helper money to buy groceries, household consumables and supplies.

While we trust our domestic helper, we do like to keep a record of where our expenses went, and ensure an adequate weekly budget is allocated. If you are like us, we settle our expenses with our domestic helper weekly, and have gradually developed our application around this pattern.

Our goal is make this process as painless as possible. With enough users and data, we will be able to help your run your household more efficiently and analyze your spending for potential savings.

For Helpers

Hate nagging your employer for money for their domestic expenses? Dipping into your own pocket to pay for your employer's expenses? Encourage your employer to use Deulu so that it is easy to notify them your are out of money. With clear expense record, there is no reason for them to question your hard work.

Are you still using pen, paper and calculator? It is very easy to make mistakes. Before we build Deulu, quite often we have found some errors in our helper's expense calculation. Sometimes, she miscalculated and understated the expenses. If we did not cross check the calculation, she would have end up paying it out of her own pocket! Do not allow yourself to make these silly mistakes.

We have carefully crafted our expense entry page to streamline the process of expense entry and weekly record keeping. Try using our application today!
